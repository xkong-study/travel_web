About Us

Badass Guides

"Badass Guides" is an innovative travel guide publisher, catering specifically to the adventurous spirit of college students embarking on senior year journeys and post-graduation explorations. Recognizing the unique needs of young travelers, they are excited to launch a new travel service on their website and mobile platforms. This service isn't just about providing insightful, engaging guides to the world's most captivating destinations; it's about seamlessly integrating the planning and booking process. With "Badass Guides," travelers can immerse themselves in detailed, relevant information about their chosen destinations and then conveniently book their adventures directly through the platform.

FAQ'S

WHAT IS THE NEW TRAVEL SERVICE OFFERED BY BADASS GUIDES?
Our new travel service combines detailed, adventurous travel guides with the convenience of booking your trip directly through our website and mobile app. Aimed at young travelers, it allows you to seamlessly plan and book your senior year or post-graduation trips in exciting destinations.
HOW CAN I BOOK A TRIP THROUGH BADASS GUIDES?
Booking a trip is easy! Just choose your desired destination from our travel guides, and you'll find an option to book your travel right there. Our platform offers various travel packages and experiences, catering to different budgets and interests.
ARE THERE SPECIAL DEALS FOR STUDENTS?
Absolutely! We offer special discounts and deals for students. Keep an eye on our website or sign up for our newsletter to stay informed about our latest student travel deals and packages.
CAN I CUSTOMIZE MY TRAVEL ITINERARY?
Yes, our service offers the flexibility to customize your itinerary. You can choose your preferred experiences, accommodations, and travel dates. Our aim is to provide a personalized adventure that aligns with your interests and schedule.
WHAT KINDS OF DESTINATIONS DOES BADASS GUIDES OFFER?
We specialize in a wide range of destinations that are popular with young travelers, including budget-friendly locations, adventure hotspots, and culturally rich urban centers. Our guides cover everything from hidden gems to must-see landmarks.
